Shakshouka

Easy shakshuka recipe

Shakshouka is a Maghrebi dish of eggs poached in a sauce of tomatoes, olive oil, peppers, onion,

and garlic, commonly spiced with cumin, paprika and cayenne pepper.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Red onions
  • Red chili
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan that has a lid
  2. Soften the onions, chilli, garlic and coriander stalks for 5 mins until soft
  3. Stir in the tomatoes and sugar, then bubble for 8-10 mins until thick
  4. Using the back of a large spoon, make 4 dips in the sauce
  5. Crack an egg into each on
  6. Put a lid on the pan, then cook over a low heat for 6-8 mins, until the eggs are done to your liking
