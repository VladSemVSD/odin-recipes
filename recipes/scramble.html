Scrambled Eggs

Perfect scrambled egg recipe

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from eggs stirred, whipped, or beaten together typically with salt, butter, oil,

and sometimes other ingredients, and heated so that they form into curds.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Preheat the pan
  3. Pour in egg mixture
  4. Pull a rubber spatula across the bottom of the pan to form large, soft curds of scrambled eggs
  5. Continue cooking over medium-low heat, folding and stirring the eggs every few seconds
  6. Remove the pan from the heat, and season to taste with salt and pepper
